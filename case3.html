Dream!N

The technical barrier to game content creation is very high. Our mission is to lower these barriers and redefine the traditional gaming experience through gen-AI.

Why This Initiative

Although today's gaming industry has produced a large amount of high-quality content, for the average game lover, it‘s still almost impossible to express their imagination through games.

Every form of content goes through a process of decentralization, just as we have seen with novels to Twitter, professional photography to Instagram, and movies to YouTube and TikTok. We believe that gaming too will reach such flourishing diversity, and we are the drivers behind.

History

Our company was established in mid-2022. In the initial months, the generative AI boom had not yet arrived, and our vision lacked a suitable technological breakthrough to support it, resulting in slow progress during this period. Even so, our capabilities and passion helped us attract investments and financial support from prominent institutions such as YC China, Brown Venture Prize, Amazon Web Services, and OpenAI.

First Attempt

After the surge in generative AI technology, we quickly integrated it into our product design. Within three quarters, we launched the beta test of our first product. It's an AI-driven game creation platform that allows users to create and share games in just minutes using only human language, with no technical barriers.

During the three-month beta test, over 3,000 games were created and experienced millions of times in total. The 7-day retention rate was 26%, with a second-week retention of 46%, and the average session time 36 minutes. Our data significantly outperformed similar competitors.

Latest Update

In our last beta, we also identified some issues. Even though we eliminated technical barriers, most users were still unable to create a complete, high-quality game. This is because designing a game is equally complex. Therefore, in our latest product, we have transformed the product from a game platform into a game itself - Echo of Mobius.

Although it's a game, as developers, we only develop the framework and set the rules. All game characters and events are still created by our players, who adventure, grow, and interact with the characters they create. This approach makes the creation of game content even more lightweight and, under a unified rule system, allows users’ creations to be better shared and experienced.

The early access of the game will be released in July, 2024.
